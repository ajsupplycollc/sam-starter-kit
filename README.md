# SAM Starter Kit

### Your AI business partner. Set up in minutes. No coding required.

---

## What Is This?

The SAM Starter Kit turns Claude into a full AI business partner that you talk to through Telegram. It manages your email, calendar, content, and tasks — all through simple text and voice messages on your phone.

**Built by [Strange Advanced Marketing](https://github.com/strangeadvancedmarketing)** — a non-developer who built this for his own business and now uses it every day.

---

## What You Get

- An AI partner you text on Telegram, anywhere, anytime
- Email management (reads, triages, drafts replies)
- Calendar management (checks schedule, creates events, sets reminders)
- Browser automation (fills forms, researches topics, navigates sites)
- Voice replies (your AI talks back to you)
- Desktop companion overlay (see what your AI is doing in real time)
- Morning briefings (email + calendar summary every morning)
- Content creation support

## How It Works

```
You (Telegram) ←→ Claude Code (your computer) ←→ Email, Calendar, Browser, Desktop
```

Your AI partner runs on your computer through Claude Code. You talk to it through Telegram on your phone. It has access to your email, calendar, and can control your browser and desktop to get things done.

## FAQ

**Do I need to know how to code?**
No. The setup is guided by your existing Claude. You don't touch any code.

**Is my data safe?**
Everything runs on your computer. Your emails, calendar, and business data never leave your machine. There's no cloud service in between.

**How much does it cost?**
The SAM Starter Kit is free. You need a Claude subscription ($20-100/month from Anthropic) and a Telegram account (free).

**Can I customize it?**
Yes. The CLAUDE.md file controls how your AI partner behaves. Edit it to match your business needs.

**What if something breaks?**
Text your AI partner on Telegram and ask it to fix itself. Seriously — it can diagnose and repair most issues.
